Biriyani Recipe

Ingredients:

    For the rice::

    • 2 cups basmati rice
    • 1 tablespoon oil
    • 4 cups water
    • 1 tablespoon salt
    • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 2 cloves
    • 1 bay leaf
    • 1 cardamom pod
    • 1 cinnamon stick

    For the chicken marinade:

    • 11 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
    • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
    • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
    • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
    • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
    • 1/4 teaspoon red chili powder
    • Salt to taste

    For the biryani masala:

    • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
    • 2 onions, thinly sliced
    • 2 tomatoes, chopped
    • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
    • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1/4 teaspoon red chili powder
    • Salt to taste

    For garnishing:

    • Chopped cilantro
    • Fried onions (optional)
    • Saffron-soaked milk (optional)

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Step: Wash and soak the rice: Rinse the basmati rice thoroughly in several changes of water until the water runs clear. Soak the rice in water for at least 30 minutes, or up to 2 hours.
  2. Step: Marinate the chicken: In a large bowl, combine the chicken pieces with the yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well and coat the chicken evenly. Marinate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 2 hours.
  3. Step: Cook the rice: Drain the soaked rice and add it to a pot with water, salt, cloves, bay leaf, cardamom, cinnamon stick, and oil. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 10-12 minutes, or until the rice is cooked through and fluffy. Drain the rice and set it aside.
  4. Step: Prepare the biryani masala: Heat the oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat. Add the onions and cook until they are golden brown and translucent, about 5-7 minutes.
  5. Step: Add the tomatoes, ginger-garlic paste, cumin, coriander, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt to the onions. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the tomatoes have softened and the spices are fragrant.
  6. Step: Add the marinated chicken to the pot and cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is no longer pink and is cooked through.
  7. Step: Layer the biryani: In a large baking dish or pot, start by spreading half of the cooked rice evenly at the bottom. Top with the chicken masala mixture, spreading it evenly. Sprinkle half of the chopped cilantro over the chicken masala.
  8. Step: Layer the remaining rice over the chicken masala and cilantro. Drizzle the saffron-soaked milk (if using) over the rice.
  9. Step: Seal and cook the biryani: Cover the baking dish or pot tightly with aluminum foil. Place it in a preheated oven at 350°F (175°C) for 20-30 minutes, or until the rice is heated through and the flavors have melded.
  10. Step: Garnish and serve: Fluff the rice with a fork. Garnish with chopped cilantro and fried onions (if using). Serve hot with raita or your favorite sides.
