Kottu Recipe

Ingredients:

    For the kottu:

    • 2-3 chopped Godamba roti (or paratha)
    • 2 eggs, beaten
    • 1 onion, chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
    • 1 green chili pepper, chopped (optional)
    • 1/2 teaspoon curry powder
    • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • Salt to taste

    For the vegetables:

    • 1 carrot, shredded
    • 1/2 cup cabbage, shredded
    • 1/2 cup leeks, chopped
    • 1/2 cup banana peppers, chopped (optional)

    For the protein (optional):

    • 1 chicken breast, cooked and chopped
    • 1/2 cup beef or mutton, cooked and chopped
    • 1/2 cup shrimp, cooked and chopped

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Step: Heat the oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Step: Add the eggs and scramble until cooked through.
  3. Step: Add the onion, garlic, ginger, and green chili pepper (if using) and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  4. Step: Add the curry powder, turmeric powder, and salt and cook for 1 minute more.
  5. Step: Add the chopped roti and stir to combine.
  6. Step: Add the vegetables and protein (if using) and stir to combine.
  7. Step: Cook until the vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes.
  8. Step: Serve hot with chopped cilantro and lime wedges.
