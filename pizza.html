Ingredients:

    For the dough:

    • 1 ½ cups (355 ml) warm water (105°F-115°F)
    • 1 packet (2 ¼ teaspoons) active dry yeast
    • 3 ¾ cups (490g) bread flour
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 ½ teaspoons salt

    For the toppings:

    • 12 ounces (340g) shredded mozzarella cheese
    • Your choice of toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, peppers, olives)

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Step: Make the dough: In a large bowl, combine the warm water, yeast, and 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Stir until the yeast is dissolved. Let stand for 5 minutes, or until the yeast is foamy.
  2. Step: Add the flour, salt, and remaining tablespoon of olive oil to the yeast mixture. Stir with a wooden spoon until a shaggy dough forms.
  3. Step: Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for 10 minutes, or until smooth and elastic. If the dough is too sticky, add a little more flour, 1 tablespoon at a time. If the dough is too dry, add a little more water, 1 teaspoon at a time.
  4. Step: Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and let rise in a warm place for 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  5. Step: Make the sauce: In a medium saucepan, combine the crushed tomatoes, olive oil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer over medium heat and cook for 15 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly.
  6. Step: Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to preheat.
  7. Step: Assemble the pizza: Punch down the dough and divide it in half. On a lightly floured surface, roll out one half of the dough into a 12-inch circle. Transfer the dough to a lightly floured pizza peel or baking sheet.
  8. Step: Spread ¼ cup of sauce over the dough, leaving a ½-inch border around the edge. Top with ½ cup of mozzarella cheese and your choice of toppings.
  9. Step: Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Step: Repeat steps 7-9 with the remaining dough and sauce.
